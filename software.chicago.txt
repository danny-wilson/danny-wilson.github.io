		<p><FONT color="red"><b>Important:</b> View <a href="omegaMap/versions.html">Bugs and version
			information</a> for the latest list of known issues and fixes.</FONT></p>
		<p>omegaMap is a program for detecting natural selection and recombination in DNA 
			or RNA sequences. It is based on a model of population genetics and molecular 
			evolution. The signature of natural selection is detected using the dN/dS ratio 
			(which measures the relative excess of non-synonymous to synonymous 
			polymorphism) and the signature of recombination is detected from the patterns 
			of linkage disequilibrium. The model and the method of estimation are described 
			in</p>
		<p>Wilson, D. J. and G. McVean (2006)<br>
			<b>Estimating diversifying selection and functional constraint in the presence of 
				recombination.</b><br>
			<i>Genetics</i> doi:10.1534/genetics.105.044917. (<a href="omegaMap/abstract.html">abstract</a>
			<a href="http://www.genetics.org/cgi/content/abstract/genetics.105.044917v1?ck=nck">
				pdf</a>)</p>

			source code.</p>
