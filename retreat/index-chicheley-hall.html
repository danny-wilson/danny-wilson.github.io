Technology champions

What will be big in the next 5 years of Genomics research? Genome editing, bioarxiv, nanopore, cloud computing, single cell sequencing, twitter, technology transfer, slack, reproducible research - debate with our technology champions. If you have an opinion, volunteer to pitch your idea during our debate

Child care

To give everyone the chance to participate we will cover the cost of additional childcare required to attend the retreat. Please get in touch.

Career Clinic

Peter Donnelly and the other PIs will be making themselves available to offer advice on subjects such as career steps and applying for funding. Please use the opportunity to approach them during the retreat, or email Danny Wilson in advance if you would like to prearrange an informal chat with them
